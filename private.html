Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026
We take your privacy seriously. Gmarket is designed for maximum anonymity when purchasing digital products.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Gmarket operates with a strong focus on user anonymity. We do not require accounts, logins, or any form of personal identification to browse or purchase digital goods (software licenses, access keys, etc.).

This Privacy Policy explains that we collect **almost no personal data** directly and how third-party payment processors handle transactions.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Physical addresses or billing/shipping details
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes (only temporary server logs)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Cookies for advertising or analytics
  • Any persistent personal identifiers

Our website is built for anonymous browsing and one-time purchases.

3. Third-Party Payment Processors

When you choose to pay, we redirect you to external providers:

  • Cryptocurrency — processed via wallet-to-wallet (no KYC required on our side)
  • Paysafecard — voucher code submitted manually; no personal data exchanged with us
  • PayPal / Credit Card — handled directly by PayPal/Stripe/etc.; we never see your card details or PayPal email

Any data you provide to these processors is governed by **their** privacy policies (links provided at checkout).

4. Data Security & Minimal Logging

We use industry-standard security (HTTPS, secure hosting) to protect our site. Temporary server logs (IP, timestamp, request) are kept only for abuse prevention and deleted automatically after short period.

No logs are linked to purchases or used for profiling.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / Other Laws)

Since we collect virtually no personal data, most rights under GDPR/CCPA/etc. do not apply. However, if any minimal data (e.g. temporary IP log) relates to you:

  • Right to access
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing

Contact us via Discord if you believe we hold identifiable data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions about privacy? Reach out on Discord.
